ABOUT SWEAT SESSION

 Sweat Session is your ultimate fitness partner, pushing you beyond your limits and helping you achieve your goals. Stay committed and embrace the journey!


 Working out is to engaging in physical activities or exercises with the goal of improving or maintaining physical fitness, health, and overall well-being. It can include a variety of activities, such as strength training, cardio, flexibility exercises, and sports. People work out for various reasons, such as building muscle, losing weight, boosting energy, improving mental health, or simply staying active.


Fun Fact: Exercise is a Energy Giver. Exercise might make you feel tired immediately after, but it actually boosts overall energy levels by improving oxygen flow and cardiovascular health. ⚡

THE WORKOUTS
CARDIO
YOGA
Jumping Jacks

A full-body workout that improves cardiovascular endurance, agility, and coordination.

High Knees

A dynamic cardio move that strengthens the lower body while boosting heart rate for an intense calorie burn.

Knee to Elbow

Engages your core and strengthens the obliques while increasing flexibility and stability through controlled movement.

Climbers

A high-intensity exercise that targets multiple muscle groups, improving stamina and enhancing cardiovascular health.

Punches

A quick, explosive movement that activates your core, shoulders, and arms while boosting endurance and strength.

Tree Pose

Enhances balance and concentration while strengthening the legs and improving posture.

Cobra Pose

Opens the chest and strengthens the back, improving flexibility in the spine and relieving tension in the shoulders.

Triangle Pose

A powerful stretch for the hamstrings, hips, and back while building strength in the legs and improving flexibility in the torso.

Warrior II

A dynamic pose that engages the legs, core, and arms while promoting strength, stability, and endurance in the lower body.

Extended Side Angle Pose

Targets the thighs, hips, and shoulders, stretching the sides of the torso while improving balance and flexibility.
